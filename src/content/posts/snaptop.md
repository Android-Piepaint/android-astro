# Snaptop

_Noun_ (plural Snaptops, Snaptii) </br>

A laptop in classic form factor or 2-in-1 detachable devices that shipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon processor.

## Etymology

A blend of _Snapdragon_ (Qualcomm's ARM-based processor designed for phones and computers) and _laptop_.

## Spanish

## Etymology

Unadapted bororrowing from English _Snaptop_.
